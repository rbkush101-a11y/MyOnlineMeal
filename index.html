Our Services

Food Catering

Food catering means professionally providing food, drinks, and service for events at a location away from a traditional restaurant, handling everything from menu planning, cooking, and delivery to setup, serving, and cleanup for gatherings like weddings, parties, or corporate meetings, allowing hosts to focus on guests. It's about customized culinary experiences outside a fixed kitchen, requiring logistics and adaptation to different venues and event themes

Bulk Ordering

Bulk catering is the large-scale production and delivery of food for big groups, focusing on efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and consistency, common in institutional settings (schools, hospitals) or large events (corporate, weddings) by preparing huge quantities at once, often with optimized kitchen workflows and wholesale supplies. It emphasizes batch cooking and streamlined processes to serve many people while maintaining quality and safety standards.

Food Ordering

An online food ordering system for restaurants lets customers order via website/app for delivery/pickup, using features like digital menus, customization, online payments, and order tracking, with solutions ranging from integrated POS systems (Oracle) managing all channels to commission-free platforms (UpMenu, Foodiv, QRmenu) for direct sales, helping boost revenue and efficiency. Key types include third-party aggregators (Zomato, Swiggy) and direct-to-consumer platforms (Flipdish, FoodChow).
